our story

Medicare provides top-tier service, selection, and pricing on emergency medical equipment and first aid supplies. Our eCommerce website is a source of comprehensive information on cardiac arrest and other medical emergencies, CPR training and supplies, and more. Our stated goal as a company is to make life-saving knowledge and equipment affordable and available to everyone. Customers can browse Medicare's large, well-organized product catalog or choose to search by product, brand, or part number.We allow customers to search based on their symptoms or the active ingredient they need.We served both specific product suggestions and more-broad category suggestions and query suggestions. To help a user quickly identify the best option.

The ease of navigation ensures all customers can find what they need to help prevent and respond to emergencies in the workplace or at home.
